Dr. Santhosh kumar Gajavally

MD (Radiology)

A brief introduction about myself , completed MD radiology in 2015. I am working a consultant radiologist in tele radiology centre , krsna diagnostics , I report wide variety of cases from different government medical colleges throughout india . Last one year I have been working in emirates hospital , dubai . Daily I report around 100 Ct and Mri cases . What I will teach is --- APPROACH . Every class will start with basics and related anatomy . I have wide variety of cases ( Nearly 2000 in my hard disc) starting from basic infract to most complicated cases . I will show real cases, tell u the approach , how to write report , what points u have to mention in each case andan origina report . Definetely u will be able to report 95 percent of cases after classes .
